Tea Leaves Salad

Snack

Tea leaf salad, La-phet, contains fermented tea leaves as its main ingredient. It is one of the authentic foods of Myanmar.

Food background

It used to be a symbol of peace and was served as a peace offering in history. Nowadays, it is a common dish offered to guests and is usually accompanied by Myanmar green tea.

Fun fact

Like coffee and cocca, "La-phet" gives caffeinated effect. In our lovely tradition, the wedding invitations are usually come together with a small pack of "La-phet", meaning that we are warmly welcomed to the ceremony.
